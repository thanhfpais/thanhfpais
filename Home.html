Welcome to Vu Resto & Bar.

Vu is exceptional food enjoyed in an inspiring space that brings out the social side of people.



Chic, understated, and local, Vu is a portal to a full-on sensory dining experience against the iconic beauty of the historic St. John’s harbour. The soundtrack is a mix of conversation, laughter and gently upbeat tunes tripping along in the background. The smells, subtly drifting in, hint at the ingenuity of a Chef with his dream job.

Every dish is innovative, with unexpected finishes. All made in house and from scratch. Including Chef Chris’s personal favourite – Maple and Mustard Seed Glazed Salmon. Every cocktail is imaginative, some with a nod to tradition, others with a more modern twist. Every day brings a new end to a meal, with an assortment of regularly changing desserts. On every level, Vu is a conversation starter.

Free parking downtown. We have that, too. Just outside our door

Are you looking for a new favourite spot? Come experience Vu restaurant. Call us at 012 345 678 to make your reservation.

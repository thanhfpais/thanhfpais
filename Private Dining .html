PRIVATE AND
CORPORATE EVENTS



Are you planning a private event? Maybe a wedding, birthday, or anniversary? Or a corporate event, like a workshop, seminar or meeting? If you are, you’re probably looking for ways to make the occasion both memorable and inspirational.


Vu occupies space over two levels and accommodates groups from 20-110. Each room boasts expansive views of the historic St. John’s harbour, downtown St. John’s. It is, simply put, the best view in the city.


Executive Chef, Chris Chafe, is an award-winning Chef known for creating menus that feature a wide variety of tantalizing dishes, made fresh and from scratch. Chef Chris looks forward to putting his talents to work for you, tailoring your special event menu to your tastes and preferences.


Event planning services include audio/visual equipment and – bonus! - free parking.
